# Sales Analysis for an E-Commerce Business

A mini project that performs exploratory data analysis (EDA) and visualization on e-commerce sales data to uncover trends, identify best-selling products, and provide actionable business insights.

---

## 📌 Objective

Analyze the sales data of an e-commerce company to:
- Uncover revenue trends over time
- Identify best-selling products and high-performing regions
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

| Category | Tools |
|---|---|
| Language | Python |
| Data Analysis | Pandas, NumPy |
| Visualization | Matplotlib, Seaborn |
| Dataset | Kaggle Sales Data |

## 🔍 Methods Used

1. **Data Cleaning** — Handled missing values, duplicates, and standardized date/text formats
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** — Descriptive statistics, trend identification, and outlier detection
3. **Visualization** — Generated charts to represent sales performance
4. **Aggregation & Grouping** — Grouped data by product, region, and time period

---

## 📊 Visualizations

| Chart | Description |
|---|---|
| Histogram | Revenue distribution across transactions |
| Pie Chart | Sales contribution by region (North 35.2%, East 28.6%, West 17.1%, South 19%) |
| Bar Chart | Sales performance per product (Product C highest, Product A lowest) |
| Line Graph | Revenue trend across months (Jan–May) |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Product Performance:** Products C and B generate the highest sales and should be prioritized in inventory and marketing efforts
- **Regional Contribution:** North and East dominate sales; South and West are underperforming and need targeted strategies
- **Revenue Trend:** Consistent growth observed Jan–Mar, with a dip in April indicating potential for seasonal promotions

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Focus marketing and inventory budget on top-performing products (C, B)
- Launch targeted campaigns in underperforming regions (South, West)
- Plan promotional offers during revenue dip periods to recover sales
